## 🏷️ **FILE NAMING THAT WORKS**

### **The Magic Formula:**
```
Vendor_YYYYMMDD_Amount.ext
```

### **Real Examples:**
| Receipt From | Date | Amount | File Name |
|--------------|------|--------|-----------|
| Starbucks | March 15, 2024 | $12.45 | `Starbucks_20240315_12.45.jpg` |
| Home Depot | March 18, 2024 | $127.89 | `HomeDepot_20240318_127.89.pdf` |
| Shell Gas | March 20, 2024 | $45.67 | `Shell_20240320_45.67.png` |

### **Pro Tips:**
- Remove spaces from vendor names (use underscore: `Burger_King`)
- Use exact amounts including cents (`12.45` not `12`)
- Date format: YYYYMMDD (20240315 for March 15, 2024)
- Any image format works: jpg, png, pdf, etc.

## 🎯 **AUTO-MATCHING: HOW IT WORKS**

### **The System is Smart:**
The photo discovery engine automatically matches photos to expenses by comparing:
- **Vendor Name** (exact or close match)
- **Date** (exact or within a few days)
- **Amount** (exact or very close)

### **Confidence Levels:**
- **90%+** = Perfect match → Auto-linked ✅
- **70-89%** = Good match → Auto-linked with note ⚠️
- **50-69%** = Review needed → You decide 🤔
- **Below 50%** = Poor match → Manual linking required ❌

### **What You'll See:**
Each linked photo shows confidence like: **"View Receipt (87% - Exact vendor/date)"**

## 🎭 **COMMON SCENARIOS**

### **"I have hundreds of old receipts"**
**Solution:** Start with recent months, work backwards when time permits. Even partial organization is better than none!

### **"My photos don't match automatically"**
**Likely causes:**
- Vendor name spelled differently (`McDonald's` vs `McDonalds`)
- Wrong date format (use YYYYMMDD)
- Amount difference (use exact amount including cents)

**Fix:** Use Manual Photo Link for problem photos.

### **"I scan everything to PDF"**
**Perfect!** The system works great with PDFs. Name them the same way: `Vendor_YYYYMMDD_Amount.pdf`

### **"I take photos with my phone"**
**Excellent!** Just rename the photos on your computer before running discovery.

## 🆘 **TROUBLESHOOTING**

- ✅ Use exact vendor names from your expenses

- ✅ Include exact amounts with decimals

### **Discovery Takes Too Long:**
- ✅ Organize photos in monthly subfolders
- ✅ Limit photos per folder to under 100
- ✅ Run discovery monthly instead of annually
