MACRS Asset Categories

Understanding the correct asset category is crucial for accurate depreciation:

  • 3-year property: Software, tools, breeding livestock
  • 5-year property: Computers, cars, trucks, office equipment
  • 7-year property: Office furniture, fixtures, agricultural machinery
  • 10-year property: Water transportation equipment, single-purpose structures
  • 15-year property: Improvements, landscaping, roads
  • 20-year property: Farm buildings, certain equipment

Section 179 & Bonus Depreciation (2024-2025)

Accelerated depreciation options can provide immediate tax benefits:

  • Section 179 (2024): Up to $1,220,000 immediate expensing
  • Phase-out threshold: Begins at $3,050,000 total purchases
  • Bonus depreciation (2024): 80% immediate deduction
  • Bonus depreciation (2025): 60% immediate deduction
  • Future years: 40% (2026), 20% (2027), 0% (2028+)
