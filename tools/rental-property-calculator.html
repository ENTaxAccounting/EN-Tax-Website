Real Estate Investment Fundamentals

Key Investment Metrics

Understanding these metrics is crucial for evaluating rental property investments:

  • Cap Rate: Net Operating Income ÷ Purchase Price (6-10% is good)
  • Cash-on-Cash Return: Annual Cash Flow ÷ Cash Invested (8-12% target)
  • 1% Rule: Monthly rent should equal 1% of purchase price
  • Gross Rent Multiplier: Purchase Price ÷ Annual Rent (lower is better)
  • Cash Flow: Income minus all expenses including debt service

Investment Strategies

Different real estate investment approaches offer varying risk and return profiles:

  • Cash Flow Focus: Prioritize monthly income over appreciation
  • Appreciation Play: Buy in growing areas for long-term gains
  • BRRRR Method: Buy, Rehab, Rent, Refinance, Repeat
  • Turnkey Rentals: Purchase move-in ready properties
  • House Hacking: Live in one unit, rent out others

Tax Benefits of Real Estate

Rental property ownership offers significant tax advantages:

  • Depreciation: Deduct property value decline over 27.5 years
  • Operating Expenses: Repairs, maintenance, management fees
  • Mortgage Interest: Deduct loan interest payments
  • 1031 Exchanges: Defer capital gains by trading properties
  • Professional Consultation: Work with tax professionals for optimization
