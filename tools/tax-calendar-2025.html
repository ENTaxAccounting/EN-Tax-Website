🛡️ Safe Harbor Rules

Avoid underpayment penalties by meeting one of these safe harbor requirements:

100% Rule Pay 100% of last year's tax (if AGI ≤ $150k)
110% Rule Pay 110% of last year's tax (if AGI > $150k)
90% Rule Pay 90% of current year's tax liability
⚠️ Penalty Avoidance

You may owe a penalty if:

  • You owe $1,000 or more in tax for 2025
  • You didn't pay at least 90% of the tax shown on your 2025 return
  • You didn't pay at least 100% (or 110%) of the tax shown on your 2024 return
  • Your quarterly payments were late

Current penalty rate: Approximately 8% annually (adjusted quarterly)

Maximize Your 2025 Tax Planning

Year-Round Tax Planning

Effective tax planning happens throughout the year, not just during tax season:

  • Track deductible expenses monthly
  • Review withholding mid-year
  • Plan year-end tax moves in November/December
  • Stay informed about tax law changes

Important Form Deadlines

Key forms and their deadlines to keep in mind for 2025:

  • W-2s and 1099s: January 31
  • Individual returns (1040): April 15
  • Business returns: March 15 (partnerships/S-corps)
  • Extensions: October 15 (individuals), September 15 (business)

Penalty Prevention

Avoid costly tax penalties with proper planning:

  • File tax returns on time, even if you can't pay
  • Make quarterly estimated payments if required
  • Keep accurate records and receipts
  • Set calendar reminders for all deadlines
