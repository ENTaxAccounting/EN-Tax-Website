Individual Tax Preparation Best Practices

Document Organization Tips

Stay organized throughout the tax year for easier preparation:

  • Create digital and physical filing systems by category
  • Save all tax-related documents in one location
  • Track deductible expenses with receipts and documentation
  • Use smartphone apps to photograph receipts immediately
  • Set up automatic savings for important tax documents

Common Missed Deductions

Don't overlook these frequently missed tax benefits:

  • State and local tax deductions (SALT)
  • Student loan interest payments
  • Charitable contributions (cash and non-cash)
  • Medical expenses above 7.5% of AGI
  • Home office deductions for remote workers

Filing Status & Credits

Optimize your tax situation with proper filing choices:

  • Choose the most beneficial filing status
  • Claim all eligible dependents and credits
  • Consider Roth IRA conversions in low-income years
  • Plan charitable giving for maximum tax benefit
  • Time major purchases for optimal tax treatment
