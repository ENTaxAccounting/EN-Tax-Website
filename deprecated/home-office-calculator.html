Maximize Your Home Office Deduction

The IRS offers two methods for calculating your home office deduction: the Simplified Method and the Actual Expense Method. Each has its advantages, and the best choice depends on your specific situation.

Use this calculator to compare both methods and discover which one saves you the most money on your taxes.
