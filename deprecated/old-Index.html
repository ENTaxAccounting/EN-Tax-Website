About E&N Tax & Accounting

Founded in 2024, E&N Tax & Accounting brings a personal touch to professional financial services. The firm specializes in serving individuals, small businesses, startups, and small portfolio real estate investors with the care and attention they deserve.

The E&N Story

E&N Tax & Accounting represents more than just a business—it's a testament to family values and professional dedication. Named after twin daughters Emma and Nina, the firm embodies the care, attention, and commitment brought to every client relationship.

The founder believes that behind every tax return is a story, a family, or a dream. That's why E&N approaches each client with the same care and attention given to family. This commitment to excellence ensures that clients receive not just accurate tax services, but a trusted partner in their financial journey.

While currently focused on providing exceptional service to existing clients, E&N is building a foundation for thoughtful growth—always maintaining the personal touch that sets the firm apart.

"When clients work with E&N, they're not just another number. They become part of an extended family, and their success is E&N's success."
