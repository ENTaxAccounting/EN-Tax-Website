Transparent Pricing Philosophy

Fair, value-based pricing that puts clients first

A Different Approach to Pricing

At E&N Tax & Accounting, the firm believes in complete transparency when it comes to pricing. That's why E&N has adopted a value-based pricing model that benefits both the firm and its clients.

Value-Based Pricing

To ensure E&N can work on clients' books in the smoothest, most efficient fashion, the firm's pricing is dependent on the size and complexity of the job. After an initial consultation, clients receive a flat rate for the services being provided.

In other words, E&N does not work with hourly rates, which is another reason why the firm makes the tax and accounting process simpler from start to finish. No more worrying about paying by the hour with no guarantee of what the final bill will cost!

At E&N Tax & Accounting, transparency is the foundation of trust. The firm believes every client deserves to know exactly what their investment will be before work begins.

💎

No Surprise Bills
